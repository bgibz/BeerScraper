4th Annual Beer League BASH

3pm to 9pm on Saturday, March 10th at the Britannia Community Ice Rink (1661 Napier Street)

More info and tickets available at www.beerleaguebash.com

$20 ENTRANCE, with 100% of proceeds going to BASH: Britannia After School Hockey. This fundraising event will include:

  • 1 FREE can of beer
  • 17 local craft breweries serving cans on the rink concourse ($4/355ml $6/473ml)
  • 4 Craft Brewery Teams battling it out on the ice in a round-robin tournament: Bomber, Parallel 49, Vancouver ‘Craft Beer All-Stars’, Suburban ‘Craft Beer All-Stars’
  • FREE Entrance to the After Party hosted by Parallel 49 at Britannia Hall (1739 Venables) across the street from the rink.

ATTENDEES CAN EARN BEER DISCOUNTS!

$1 Off will be given for every clean, gently-used hockey item donated to the BASH program (I.E. pair of elbow pads, helmet, socks, sticks, etc.)
